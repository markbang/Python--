宗旨：
The iConference series is presented by the iSchools, a worldwide association of Information Schools 
dedicated to advancing the information field, and preparing students to meet the information challenges of the 21st Century.
Affiliation with the iSchools is not required—all information scholars, researchers and practitioners are welcome at the iConference.
